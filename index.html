About Me

Hello, I am currently a third-year student majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics at HKUST. I am an intern at RenLab, led by Yi R. (May) Fung.

My research interests lie in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and multimodality. Specifically, I am interested in exploring interpretable methods to guide (vision-)language models' behavior and make them more reliable tools for solving real-world problems. I am also interested in discovering the limitations and boundaries of current models through explainable benchmarking.
