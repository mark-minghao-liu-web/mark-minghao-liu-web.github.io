About Me

Hello, I am currently a final year undergraduate majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics at HKUST.

My research interests lie in Natural Language Processing (NLP), with a broader focus on building interpretable, reliable, and socially aware AI systems. Specifically, I am interested in:

  1. Developing interpretable methods to guide the behavior of (vision-)language models and enhance their reliability in real-world applications.
  2. Diagnosing the limitations and boundaries of current models through explainable and systematic benchmarking.
  3. Modeling social and economic phenomena—such as public policy and financial markets—through (multi-)agent simulations.
  4. Designing dynamic and scalable learning environments, together with robust evaluation metrics, to enable LLM agents to learn, reason, and perform beyond human-level competence.

I also have experience in formal language (e.g., Lean) and neural circuit modeling.
